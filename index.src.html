I am currently a PhD student enrolled at Lancaster University but situated at CERN. My research surrounds superconducting crab cavities for the High Luminosity Large Hadron Collider (HL-LHC) upgrade. Specifically I am involved with both simulations and measurements of Higher Order Modes (HOMs) and multipole components.


This site

This website was set-up as a project to both improve my Python/HTML skills whilst also providing an area for cataloguing information about myself, documentation and projects.

The site is generated using Nikola, which is built by Python.

Skills

I am an RF/Electronics Engineer with skills in RF design (cavities and filters) and RF measurements. During my projects and experience thus far, I have been part of the design, build and testing procedures. I most enjoy the challenges associated with bringing a concept to reality and have developed a lot of experience in 'desgin-for-manufacture'.

As well as my personal software, measurement and programming skills (detailed in my CV), I enjoy collabaoration and learning from others and believe this aspect can be equally as important as technical profficieneis.
