I am currently a PhD student enrolled at Lancaster University but situated at CERN. My research surrounds superconducting crab cavities for the High Luminosity Large Hadron Collider (HL-LHC) upgrade. Specifically I am involved with both simulations and measurements of Higher Order Modes (HOMs) and multipole components.


This site

This website was set-up as a project to both improve my Python/HTML skills whilst also providing an area for cataloguing information about myself, documentation and projects.
